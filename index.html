DECISION-MAKER VALIDATION

03
AI Voice Agent Dials Out
Validates contact, identifies the decision-maker, qualifies interest, routes to the right next step.
AI Agent: "Hi, this is Sarah from AML Smart Guard. I'm calling about the new AML compliance regulations coming into effect for [industry] businesses. Could I speak with [contact name] please?"

// Gatekeeper scenario

Receptionist: "They're not available right now."
AI Agent: "No problem — could I get the best time to reach them? Or I can send through a quick summary by email if that's easier."
